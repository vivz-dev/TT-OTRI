List of DSDriver Related Known Issues Fixed in Release, Mod and Fix Pack

The list of Known Issues are organized based on version of DSDriver. Further the list of Known Issues are separated based on below three categories:

JCC Driver
- Known Issues related to JCC/JDBC, SQLJ, CLPPlus and NoSQL/JSON driver
.NET Driver
- Known Issues related to C# .NET application development
ODBC and CLI Driver
- Known Issues related to ODBC/CLI, OLEDB, ESQL and other components used by C/C++ application development

A detailed list of Known Issues of previous releases can be found in "JDBC Driver", "Client Interfaces", "Client" and "CLI" section of the given link here.
